
abstract=The Reac4Cat is an Ontology intended to save, store and inference relations for Reaction experiments which were performed with or without a catalytic material in the experimental. The Ontology inferences the reactiontype and the corresponding reaction components based on an internal store of documented reactions, a reasoner (HermiT was used for testing this) and OWL2 logic manly focused arround General Class Axions/ Right hand side logic.
